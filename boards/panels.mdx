## What Are Panels?

Panels are modular components that display alongside your board:

- View detailed contact information
- Take notes and add context
- View and send emails
- Chat with the AI Board Agent

## Available Panel Types

### Notes Panel

Add and view notes for contacts.

**Features:**
- Rich text notes
- Timestamped entries
- Persistent across sessions

### Emails Panel

View email history and create drafts for contacts.

**Features:**
- See emails exchanged with contact's company domain
- Create new email drafts
- Reply to existing threads
- Opens in Gmail for sending

## Using Panels

### Opening Panels

1. Click on a contact card on the board
2. The detail view opens with panel tabs
3. Click panel tabs to switch between views

### Adding Panels

1. Click the **+** button in the panel tabs
2. Select the panel type to add
3. Panel is added to the contact view

### Panel Navigation

Navigate between different panels by clicking the tabs at the top of the panel area.
