## Managing Integrations

### Viewing Connected Integrations

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
2. View all available and connected integrations
3. Manage connection status

### Disconnecting Integrations

To disconnect an integration:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
2. Find the integration
3. Click **Disconnect** or **Remove**
4. Confirm

<Warning>
Disconnecting an integration stops data sync. Historical data typically remains in Pockla.
</Warning>
